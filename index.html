About

I am a non-traditional student having returned to school after 15 years to study Visual and Media arts at the University of Montana. I am inspired by my surroundings, by the breathtaking beauty of the Northwest and Montana landscapes, but I also have a passion for traveling abroad. I surround myself with bright bold colors in the indigo-blue and jade green spectrum.

I'm a self-published author. My pen name is Ava Clary. This is not a secret, because I keep telling people about it. I blog about writing and my books at AvaClary.com and So Easily Distracted

Why me?

Balance is important to me. Coding requires a balance between organizing and paying attention to details and a more creative aspect. To me that means following my intuition and trusting in my creative inpulses, but also knowing when to adapt. Paying attention to small details is especially important for coding, since one small error can effect your entire project.
